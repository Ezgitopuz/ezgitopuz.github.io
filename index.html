Ezgi Topuz Buldanli

PhD Candidate · Operations Research & Logistics

Wageningen University & Research

I am a PhD researcher specializing in agent-based modeling and simulation, with a focus on behavioral dynamics and decision-making in complex systems. My research currently centers on modeling heterogeneous user decisions in freight matching platforms, examining how individual choices and interactions shape market-level outcomes.

I am passionate about leveraging computational modeling and behavioral theory to understand how decisions emerge, and how system-level outcomes arise from individual interactions. Beyond logistics, my broader interest lies in modeling human decision-making across socio-economic systems, with the ambition of building adaptive, and policy-relevant simulations.
